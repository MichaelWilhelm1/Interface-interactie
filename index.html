SUPER GOOMBA TIMELINE

SUPER MARIO BROS

1985

Goombas are one of the major species of the Mario franchise. Since their first game appearance in Super Mario Bros they have become the most iconic and common enemies in the entire Mario franchise followed by Koopa Troopas. They resemble small brown mushrooms with two feet and no visible arms or legs.

SUPER MARIO BROS 3

1988

Goombas can be found in every world except Dark Land. These Goombas are unchanged in behavior from their appearances in Super Mario Bros. This game introduces the concept of Goomba variants such as Para-Goombas, winged Goombas that either fly and drop Micro-Goombas if it is tan or simply hop if it is red.
